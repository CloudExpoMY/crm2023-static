CRM Trial Connect 2023

CRM Trial Connect is now on its 2nd year, with more interesting program content developed for participating clinical research stakeholders! True to its form in being a one-stop event for multinational clinical trials in Malaysia, the program showcases Malaysia’s capabilities, experiences and achievements in clinical research.

This year’s conference is more special as it is also held in conjunction with the Clinical Trials Day celebration! With Sponsored Research Awards to be presented and clinical research stakeholders tributed, the CRM Trial Connect 2023 will host a bevy of interesting talks, dialogues and presentations from study investigators, patient support groups, industry members and clinical research centres.

Session Highlights include:

  • Clinical Research Excellence: Sharing by Top Investigators and Sites
  • Industry & Investigator Dialogues
  • Global Networks of Excellence
  • Tapping into the Potential of Primary Healthcare and Private Centres
  • Patient Engagement
  • Insights into Clinical Trials’ Design & Conduct
  • Innovations in Clinical Trials

We welcome interested parties to participate in this conference as it provides an excellent networking platform to explore new opportunities in clinical trial conduct in Malaysia. Do register and reserve your seat now!

ABOUT

Clinical Research Malaysia

Established by Malaysian Ministry of Health in 2012, Clinical Research Malaysia (CRM) exists to advance global health solutions for a brighter, more hopeful future for the people by providing speedy and reliable end-to-end clinical research support for quality studies.

Clinical Research Malaysia is ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System) & ISO 37001:2016 (Anti-Bribery Management System) certified organisation. For more information about CRM, do head to www.clinicalresearch.my.
